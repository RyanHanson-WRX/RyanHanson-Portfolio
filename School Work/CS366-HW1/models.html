Subaru WRX STi Base Models

Impreza:
The Subaru Impreza is the base model, in which both the WRX and the WRX STi are built off of. The Impreza itself is a standard sedan/hatchback car with no sporty features but good for a daily commuter.
WRX:
The Subaru WRX is a step above the Subaru Impreza, the difference between the WRX and the Impreza would be the sporty features. The WRX is a 5-speed manual (or automatic), turbocharged version of the Impreza.
STi:
The Subaru WRX STi is an even more sporty version of the base WRX. It has a bigger engine, more powerful turbo, stronger suspension, and only comes in a 6-speed manual. The STi can easily be distinguished from a WRX as the STi comes equipt with a sporty race wing on the trunk.
