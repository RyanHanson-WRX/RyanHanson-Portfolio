Subaru WRX STi Rally History

Subaru had been putting their skills to the test in the 1970's when their Subaru Leone was racing in the Australia's Southern Cross Rally. Fast forward, after Subaru gained a victory at the Safari Rally in 1990, they put their next target on the World Rally Championship. In 1993, the first win for the STi Legacy was achieved in the New Zealand Rally. The following year would produce the first Subaru Impreza WRC would be developed, creating a strong foundation for the now Subaru WRX STi.

Top Subaru Rally Drivers

  1. Colin McRae; 16 wins
  2. Peter Solberg; 13 wins
  3. Richard Burns; 8 wins
  4. Carlos Sainz; 4 wins
  5. Juha Kankkunen; 2 wins
  6. Kenneth Eriksson; 2 wins
  7. Tommi Mäkinen; 1 win
  8. Piero Liatti; 1 win
