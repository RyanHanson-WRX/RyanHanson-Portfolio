Subaru WRX STi Engine

The Subaru WRX STi is a boxer engine, meaning it's a flat 4 cylinder. It's a 2.5L, turbo-charged beast of an engine. On stock equiptment, it makes around 305hp at 4,000-5,000 rpms, produces 295 ft-lbs torque, and about 20 PSI of boost. There are drive options which include: Intelligent, Sport, and Sport Sharp, in the order of least power to most power output. The stock 0-60 MPH time of this mean machine is around 4.6 seconds.
