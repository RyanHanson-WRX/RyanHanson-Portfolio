What is a Subaru WRX STi?

A Subaru WRX STi is a sports/rally car with 305hp, 290lb-ft torque, and about 20lbs of boost.

What does the WRX and the STi stand for?

The 'WRX' stands for 'World Rally eXperimental', and the 'STi' stands for 'Subaru Tecnica International'

How much does a Subaru WRX STi cost?

For a 2021 Subaru WRX STi, Edmunds lists a new model at $38,170. Although, you can always find a used model for cheaper.
